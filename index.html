Privacy Policy for ChatCHK

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

ChatCHK is a browser extension that allows users to analyze Facebook posts using the OpenAI API. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

  • Post Content: When you click “Analyze,” the extension may collect the visible text of a Facebook post or the text you have highlighted. This content is sent to the OpenAI API for analysis.
  • No Personal Accounts Data: ChatCHK does not collect, store, or access your Facebook login credentials, personal messages, or private account data.
  • Local Storage: Your analysis results, preferences, and any tags you add may be stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local.

2. How We Use the Information

  • Analysis: Post content is sent to OpenAI to generate summaries and accuracy assessments.
  • Replies & Tagging: If you use the reply or tagging features, the relevant text is stored locally or (in future versions) may be sent to our servers for community tagging.
  • No Sale of Data: ChatCHK does not sell, rent, or trade any user data.

3. Sharing of Information

  • With OpenAI: Only the selected post text is sent to the OpenAI API for processing. OpenAI’s usage is governed by their Privacy Policy.
  • No Other Third Parties: We do not share your data with advertisers or other companies.

4. Data Retention

Analysis results stored locally remain until you clear your browser’s extension data. If future cloud tagging is implemented, retention details will be updated and clearly disclosed.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information processed through the extension. However, no method of transmission over the internet or storage system is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through an update to the extension.
